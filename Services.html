Farm Stay Accommodation 


The farmhouse at Rose Creek Farm comfortably accommodates up to 10 people and is strategically positioned in an elevated location, allowing visitors to bask in the breathtaking scenery of the farm. The house is equipped with various facilities to ensure a pleasant stay:


  • Laundry facilities and linen will be provided for convenience.
  • Enjoy the comfort of fully ducted air conditioning during your stay.
  • Warm up by the cozy wood fire on cooler evenings.
  • Children and adults alike will love the large enclosed play area.
  • A spacious open gathering area is perfect for group activities and socializing.
  • Guests can avail of the under-cover car park for their vehicles.
  • Experience delightful barbecues with the provided BBQ setup.
  • A light breakfast will be offered to start your day with a delicious touch.
  • The farmhouse boasts four well-appointed bedrooms for a restful night's sleep.
  • The accommodation comfortably caters to a group of 10 people.
  • Stay connected with complimentary Wi-Fi access throughout your stay.

With these amenities and the serene beauty of the farm, guests are in for a memorable and enjoyable experience at Rose Creek Farm

 

Farm facilities 

Animals feeding

Guests can experience the joy of interacting with friendly farm animals, such as sheep, ​alpacas, chickens, and ducks, by feeding them under the guidance of our farm staff. 

Bird feeding 

 Enjoy the enchanting company of various bird species as you feed them, creating memorable moments and connecting with nature's winged inhabitants. 

Farm trucking

Embark on adventurous farm trucking to explore the vast farmland, taking in the sweeping landscapes and immersing yourself in the rural charm.

Kids Play Ground

Children can have endless fun and let their imaginations soar at our specially designed play area, equipped with swings, slides, and other exciting play equipment. 

Bicycle Ride Around the Farm

Guests can take in the natural beauty of the farm at their own pace by exploring its scenic trails and pathways on bicycles, offering a delightful and eco-friendly way to experience the farm's charm 

Campfire

Gather around the crackling campfire in the evenings to share stories and enjoy the warmth, creating a cozy and unforgettable experience for guests of all ages.
